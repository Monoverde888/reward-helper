What is Reward Helper?

We are a support team for Discord servers.

We are an expert team, which in the beginning got a streak of suprising servers to configure.

We are different and not just because we are yet another team; we are different, and it is simply our taste for perfection.

We give you the configuration of a server for you, in an essential and incomparable way.

Apart from all that, we have created our own bot, and we work with more bot creators.

However, we also raffle things every day on our server, to support people in our community.
